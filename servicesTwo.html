LinkedIn Learning for Higher Education

Through data-driven analytics and customized learning content, LinkedIn Learning partners with higher ed to supplement curricula and position students to obtain the careers of tomorrow, while also advancing the skills of faculty, staff, and alumni.

Increasingly, higher education is focused on helping students get good jobs after graduation, and stand out in a global economy.

With LinkedIn Learning, along with providing on-demand online learning on the latest softwares and skills, you also position your students to grow their brand on the world's largest professional network.
