Global Talent Trends 2020

4 trends changing the way you attract and retain talent

Download report now

In this new decade, recruiting and HR will look a little different than they once did. To ensure your talent strategy reflects this evolution, it might be time for an update.

To help you identify where to invest your time and resources, the Global Talent Trends 2020 report delves into the four biggest trends that will change how you hire and retain talent this year.

  • Why employee experience  may be the new face of HR
  • How to use people analytics to drive better decision-making
  • Why internal recruiting is making a huge comeback
  • How to make the most of a multigenerational workforce

You’ll also find tips, insights, and strategies to help you navigate these trends—based on a survey of more than 7,000 talent professionals in 35 countries, LinkedIn data, and 40 interviews with experts. 

Start the new decade on the right foot by refining your strategy today.
